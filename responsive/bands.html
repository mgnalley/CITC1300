Bands inspired by Rush

Rush has been a very influential band to many others, whether also prog rock or not.

Bands

Primus

Les Claypool, the bassist and frontman of the band Primus, was heavily influenced by Geddy Lee's finesse and techniques.

Ninja Sex Party

Although not always as evident, NSP has been heavily influenced by Rush. The band's frontman, Dan Avidan, has been a huge fan of theirs for years and is constantly seen making references and wearing Rush shirts. NSP has done quite a few covers of Rush songs, such as Subdivisions and Closer to the Heart. In one of their most recent albums, the Prophecy, the ballad like stories told feels reminiscent to Rush's infamous album 2112.

Smashing Pumpkins

Billy Corgan has admitted to being influenced by Rush, as their song Cherub Rock is clearly inspired by Rush's 'By-Tor and the Snow Dog'.

Foo Fighters

Both Dave Grohl and late Taylor Hawkins of Foo Fighters have admitted that Rush, especially their hit song 2112, was a big inspiration for their music.

Metallica

Geddy Lee almost produced Metallica's album "Master of Puppets", and has been a heavy influence on the band as a whole, especially on Lars Ulrich.
